    About

    Complacency is the backdoor to terrorism - take responsibility. Safehaven is an interactive installation that probes into complacent attitudes and what that means for our defence against terrorism.

    My safety in the hands of strangers

    Singapore is safe. We don’t expect to be pickpocketed, robbed, assaulted when walking home late at night. We trust that our community will not endeavour to do that onto us. But trust can sometimes give rise to complacency, where we assume that crimes & terror will not happen in Singapore, and we lower our vigilance and awareness. The laissez faire attitude that terror will not strike on the lands of Singapore is the perfect flaw for terrorism.

    We trust our community, but we must also be vigilant against lurking dangers.


    Blanket forts are heaven built on sand

    Blanket forts are comfortable. They have pillows, blankets, warm lights, sometimes you bring your laptop in. Blankets forts are also built of makeshift items – vulnerable, just like how our society can fall if we are not vigilant enough.

    Danger on screen & all around you

    The fort is cozy and great until the lights adruptly flicker. There’s an incessant beeping somewhere behind you. Then, the Spongebob cartoon flashing on screen glitches to clips to terrorism happening around the world.
