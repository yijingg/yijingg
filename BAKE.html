    Brief

    BAKE is well-known for its mouth-watering Hokkaido Cheese Tarts - freshly baked - which is what gives that rich and addictive taste. Redesign their website to convey essence of BAKE better.

    View BAKE's revamped website.

    Approach

    I adopted a clean design style to give the images the most spotlight, highlighting BAKE’s signature tarts & their enticing taste as the key visual throughout - always tempting the user as they explore the site.

    As BAKE is unique for their cheese tarts being freshly baked and does not offer online purchase/delivery of any sort, the footer of each page instead prompts the user to find a store nearest to them.
