    Brief

    The experience of swimming at the Jellyfish Lake is nothing short of magical and out-of-this-world. Translate this experience to an memorable online web experience.

    Explore the online lake over here!

    Approach

    To translate the surrealness of swimming in a lake with Jellyfish, I utilised vibrant and saturated images, along with hand lettered typefaces to convey the magic and energy of the experience.

    Using parallax scrolling and animations, I created the experience on-screen. You are submerged (figuratively) as you scroll through floating jellyfishes. The message is at the end with recommendations to visit Jellyfish Lake in person to experience it for themselves.
